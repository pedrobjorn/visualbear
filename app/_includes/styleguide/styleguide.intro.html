Styleguide

Welcome to the website styleguide. Here you will find a collection of all the individual components and rules, used to build this site. I use a modified, custom version of Pattern Lab's atomic design structure for my site, customised by me.

The structure is divided into:

  • Atoms
  • Molecules
  • Organisms

The purpose of exposing this styleguide is to present my portfolio itself as part my portfolio work. What you see is what I use as a reference as I build the site, as it's imperative to good web design and development.
